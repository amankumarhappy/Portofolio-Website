# Product Overview

This is a personal portfolio website for **Aman Kumar Happy**, Founder & CEO of Mediokart.

## Purpose

Showcase professional achievements, projects, skills, and experience with an immersive, modern web experience featuring 3D graphics and smooth animations.

## Key Features

- **Hero section** with 3D animated background and profile
- **About, Skills, Projects, Experience, Blogs, Vision, Contact** sections
- **Smooth scroll** experience with Lenis
- **Custom cursor** interactions
- **3D graphics** powered by Three.js/React Three Fiber
- **Responsive design** optimized for all devices

## Target Audience

Recruiters, potential collaborators, investors, and anyone interested in Aman's work in healthtech and entrepreneurship, particularly focused on rural India solutions.
